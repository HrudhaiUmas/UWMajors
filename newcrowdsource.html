HELP US HELP YOU!

We aim to make our service accessible and intuitive, and use data collected exclusively from departmental offices at the UW and student submissions.

  • Provide Accurate Information: Ensure that the information you provide is accurate and reliable. Double-check facts before submitting them to avoid spreading misinformation.

  • DO Report Issues: If you come across inappropriate content or encounter any technical issues, report them promptly to the platform administrators.

  • DO Provide Feedback: Offer constructive feedback on the platform's features, usability, and improvements. This helps the organizers enhance the crowdsourcing experience.

  • Help out your fellow Huskies!
  • DON'T Submit False Information: Avoid submitting misleading or false information, as it can negatively impact the project's credibility and integrity.

  • DON'T Engage in Spamming: Refrain from spamming the platform with irrelevant or repetitive contributions.
