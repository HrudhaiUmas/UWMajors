FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is UW Majors?

UWMajors is a comprehensive website aimed at demystifying major constrained majors at the University of Washington (UW) Seattle campus. The primary goal of UWMajors is to provide students with valuable insights into the acceptance rates for different majors.
