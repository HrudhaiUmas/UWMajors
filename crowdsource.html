UW MAJORS!

Your unique experiences, perspectives, and knowledge can greatly contribute to this initiative. Whether you're a student, alumni, faculty, or simply someone who cares about higher education, we invite you to share your insights with us.

Take 2-3 minutes to help your fellow Huskies!

Do's

  • Be Honest: Provide truthful information based on your personal experiences and insights.
  • Be Detailed: Include specific details where possible to give depth and context to your answers.
  • Review Before Submitting: Double-check your responses for accuracy before hitting the submit button.

    • Dont's

  • Don't Rush: Take your time to fully consider each question before responding.
  • Don't Provide Personal Identifiers: Avoid sharing any personally identifiable information to maintain your privacy.
  • Don't Assume: Try not to guess or make assumptions about information you're unsure of, instead leave it blank.
