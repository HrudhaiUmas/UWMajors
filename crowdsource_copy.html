Welcome to UW Major, a groundbreaking project that aims to demystify competitive majors at the University of Washington (UW). We understand that choosing a major can be an overwhelming and challenging process, especially when faced with the pressure of limited spots and intense competition. Our mission is to provide students with comprehensive and transparent information about the most sought-after majors at UW, ensuring they can make informed decisions about their academic journey.
